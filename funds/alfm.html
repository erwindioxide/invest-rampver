ALFM Mutual Funds

by BPI Investment Management, Inc.

The ALFM Mutual Funds are open-end investment companies registered with, and regulated by, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). As of December 2016, the aggregate Net Asset Value of the funds amounted to about P104 Billion.

To date, the ALFM Mutual Funds have the largest market share in the mutual funds industry in the Philippines.
